# Ward Cleaning Application

Welcome to the Ward Cleaning Application! This system is designed to help organize, manage, and track cleaning assignments for church wards. The application allows administrators to create and assign cleaning tasks, while members can view their assignments, track their progress, and participate in a community-focused cleaning initiative.

The Dashboard is your central hub for quickly accessing important information. Here you can see upcoming cleaning events, recent announcements, and your personal progress. The Dashboard provides a visual overview of your assigned tasks, completion rates, and any urgent items requiring your attention.
